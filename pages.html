Privacy Policy for Hiru News

Last updated: DD Month YYYY

Welcome to Hiru News (the “App”, “We”, “Us”, or “Our”). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and disclose your personal data when you use our services (such as reading/moderating posts) and how you can exercise your rights.

1. What Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Comments or posts you submit (text, images, media).
  • Any error reports, feedback, or user support messages you send us (which may include your email address).
  • Account data (if you register)—for example, name, email, profile picture, etc.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Technical/log data such as IP address, browser type and version, device type, operating system, and user agent string.
  • The time and date you accessed the service, how long you stayed, pages visited.
  • Usage metrics and analytics (how users interact with the app) through services like Google Analytics or similar.
  • Cookies or similar local storage to enhance user experience, sessions, remember preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data for purposes including, but not limited to:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the app functionality.
  • To moderate content (e.g. reading posts/comments, flagging or hiding inappropriate content using AI).
  • To send you updates, notices, or respond to your requests or feedback.
  • To analyze usage trends, monitor performance, and diagnose technical issues.
  • To enforce our terms and comply with applicable laws and regulations.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties. We may share data in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers or contractors who assist with operations (e.g. hosting, analytics, moderation), under confidentiality agreements.
  • If required by law, legal process, or governmental request to comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect rights, property, or safety of Hiru News, our users, or others.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified if control changes).

4. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law. If you delete your account or request removal of data, we will anonymize or delete your data from active systems (though backups may persist for a limited time).

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, local storage, and similar technologies for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Remembering user preferences
  • Analytics and usage tracking
  • Security and fraud prevention

You may refuse cookies via your browser settings, though that may limit functionality of certain features.

6. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction and applicable laws, you may have rights such as:

  • Access — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — you may request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — you can ask us to delete your data (subject to some legal exceptions).
  • Restriction — you may limit how we use or process your data.
  • Object — you may object to certain forms of processing (e.g. profiling).
  • Portability — if applicable, you may request we transfer your data to another provider in structured form.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it (without affecting processing before withdrawal).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the information given below.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures (technical, administrative, organizational) to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no system is 100% secure—and we cannot guarantee absolute safety.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 (or minimum age in respective jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect or solicit information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us to delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

If our operations or service providers are located in countries outside your jurisdiction, your data may be transferred internationally. We ensure that such transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards (e.g. standard contractual clauses, encryption, etc.).
